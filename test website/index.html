Products and Service

Established in 2011, Chito N' Neneng Food Inc. is a locally trusted provider of authentic Filipino frozen goods, proudly serving the Muntinlupa community. Located in the heart of Alabang, we specialize in delivering traditional Filipino flavors that remind our customers of home-cooked meals.
